# Storehouse

![Storehouse Promo art](https://i.ibb.co/sjBVKDc/1teserex-promo-black-bars-CORRECT.png)

An old abandoned facility that was used to store various things. Some things are configurable.

FEATURES

Roughly like 23 tiles I think. <br>
Posters! They spawn a few times and they like show up in different spots.<br>
Also has an apparatus for all your apparatusing needs.<br>
Did you know the game calls the apparatus both "apparatus" and "apparatice"?<br>
Elevator.<br>
Now with an 100% chance for an entrance room!<br>
Changes theme based off of certain level tags (only ocean for now)<br>

KNOWN ISSUES:

- None, please let me know :)

- GISH50 - 50 weight<br>
- GISH150 - 150 weight<br>
- GISH300 - 300 weight<br>
- GISH900 - 900 weight (Only use if you want it to be the primary interior)<br>

# Drains

A large water treatment complex, that is weaved between multiple systems of drainage tunnels. Please give me feedback.

FEATURES

- Roughly like 28 tiles. <br>
- Features multiple massive tunnel systems, which you need to progress through to reach the rest of the facility.<br>
- Basically two interiors in one.<br>
- Finally an interior WITHOUT an apparatus.<br>
- Probably my best interior.

BONUS TAG STUFF

- Redlight (turns the lights in the tunnels from green to red).<br>
- Murica or Factory (turns the water into oil). <br>
- Pinkwater (turns the water pink). <br>
- Gore, Bloody, Flesh, Living, or Organic (turns the water into blood). <br>
- Dry (turns the water off). <br>

- None, please let me know :)
