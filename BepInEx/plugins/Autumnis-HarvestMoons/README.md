# Harvest Moons
A mod that plans to add 7 new moons for people to explore!

Currently includes 5 moons.

# Sierra
>DESIGNATION: 131 - Sierra

>RISK LEVEL: B+

>POPULATION: Abandoned

>CONDITIONS: Mountainous, arid. Fractured by large-scale mining operations over valuable minerals commonly found beneath the surface.

>FAUNA: Ruled by Leo caecus. Other fauna tends to show heavily territorial behaviors.

>POSSIBLE WEATHER: Mild, Rainy, Stormy, Foggy

>ROUTING COST: 0

![Sierra](https://i.ibb.co/ZLVwjg5/1966720-20240917200248-1.png)

# Fray
>DESIGNATION: 132 - Fray

>RISK LEVEL: B+

>POPULATION: Abandoned

>CONDITIONS: Heavily forested, severely overgrown. Landing spot located nearby the site of an old nature reserve.

>FAUNA: Heavily diverse ecosystem, fauna shows a wide variety of behaviors. Activity skyrockets at night.

>POSSIBLE WEATHER: Mild, Rainy, Stormy, Foggy, Eclipsed

>ROUTING COST: 0

![Fray](https://i.ibb.co/m8qLSt0/1966720-20240722155611-1.png)

# Cambrian
>DESIGNATION: 96 - Cambrian

>RISK LEVEL: A

>POPULATION: None

>CONDITIONS: Oceanic, few landmasses poke out through the waves.

>FAUNA: Primarily organic lifeforms call this moon home.

>POSSIBLE WEATHER: Mild, Stormy, Eclipsed

>ROUTING COST: 225

![Cambrian](https://i.ibb.co/gJvWHZ2/1966720-20240722160550-1.png)

# Temper
>DESIGNATION: 133 - Temper

>RISK LEVEL: S

>POPULATION: Abandoned

>CONDITIONS: Constant blizzards, rocky cliffs and dead forests.

>FAUNA: Minimal biological life inhabits the former residences.

>POSSIBLE WEATHER: Mild, Rainy, Stormy, Eclipsed

>ROUTING COST: 650

![Temper](https://i.ibb.co/PsyJcCt/1966720-20240722160507-1.png)

# Prominence
>DESIGNATION: 293 - Prominence

>RISK LEVEL: S

>POPULATION: None

>CONDITIONS: Dying marshland. Abandoned shipping freighter crashed on surface.

>FAUNA: Biological shipment located inside.

>POSSIBLE WEATHER: Mild, Foggy, Flooded, Eclipsed

>ROUTING COST: 900

![Prominence](https://i.ibb.co/S6WFGms/1966720-20250113064532-1.png)

### I recommend not putting custom interiors on prominence unless you have seen the gimmick and tested it.
