**Kenji's Moons** is a moon pack that has all my past, present and future moons bundled. In the future progression may be added so stay tuned!<br>
<br>
This pack contain a total of **12 moons** as or right now, all of them available from the start with different levels of difficulty and environments.<br>

### Please make sure that you don't have ["Kenjis Moons Taldor Saga"](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/rectorado/Kenjis_Moons_Taldor_Saga/) nor any of the standalone moons when you download this pack!
### All the moons have a standalone version, you can [check them out and my other mods here!](https://thunderstore.io/c/lethal-company/p/rectorado/)

*"Greed drives us crazy, makes us gamble with things bigger than what we bargain for, pushing to the stars and beyond. And when playing god isn't enough it makes us wonder... where's the limit?"*

**476-Taldor** is a lush and humid exoplanet with vast lakes were territorial creatures lurk, a vanilla flavoured planet that used to be the base of operations for Taldor Industries, now just a few cities remain where the long lost relatives of former workers still live. Even with the facilities no longer working, the rogue security robots and local fauna may kill your quota short.
Taldor is a 0 cost moon that aims to be a mid-tier moon.

## Disclamer
This moon has a gimmick that may break some functionalities in custom interiors. Since I am not able to test every single one of them, if you notice gamebreaking behaviour in a certain interior please [let me know](https://discord.com/channels/1168655651455639582/1392978985872261220) (It shouldn't be common). You can disable this gimmick in the configuration.<br>
<br>
For now the interior that has it's main gameplay feature broken is LC_Office. (You may want to blacklist this interior for this moon if playing with the gimmick activated)<br>
