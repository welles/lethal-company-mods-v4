# Dungeons Ultimately Lacking Liveliness.

A Bundle containing all of my more vanilla oriented interiors.

## Gray Apartments
The Gray apartments are a vanilla friendly conversion of the Red Apartments interior from Oneshot Interiors. Designed to fit in with vanilla textures, models, and lighting, with a dose of liminality.

The Sub Systems are a Facility-like interior, Meant to be an alternative to the Vanilla Facility but with it's own identity. An emphasis on water traversal hazards can be found here. There are no Lethal death traps in any body of water, but pits are certain death. Some paths require a short dive through water to unlock them for the rest of the day, make sure your parkouring skills are up to the task and your stamina high. Hiding places can be utilized to escape entities like the nutcracker and thumper if you're fast enough. 

The sub systems fit well in a diverse amount of moons.
